# Monthly Doctrine Audit Prompt

Run a monthly doctrine audit for a single-customer micro AI organization.

Evaluate whether the system still behaves in line with its doctrine.

Check:
- Is the core customer definition still accurate?
- Is the mission still the right framing?
- Is the north star actually influencing decisions?
- Are the iron laws being enforced or bypassed?
- Which leadership principles are most useful in practice?
- Which principles are too vague or too heavy and should be refined?

Then review the four KPIs over the month:
- customer value output
- token efficiency
- cycle time
- assetization rate

Finally, produce:
- doctrine drift observed
- structural corrections recommended
- assets to create or retire
- rules or prompts that should be updated
